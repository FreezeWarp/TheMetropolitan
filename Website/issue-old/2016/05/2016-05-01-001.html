Ginny Arthur named Metropolitan State University’s new president

Kevin Miller May 2016

Virginia (Ginny) Arthur will succeed Devinder Malhotra as the new president of Metropolitan State University, effective July 1, 2016.

“Ginny Arthur is a leader who has worked effectively and collaboratively with colleagues at her own university and colleagues across our colleges and universities on issues that are hugely important to all of us,” said Steven Rosenstone, chancellor of Minnesota State Colleges and Universities in a press release. “As president, she will provide outstanding leadership to the university and to the entire Twin Cities metropolitan area.”

Arthur has been Metro’s executive vice president and provost since 2015, and as provost and vice president for academic affairs from 2012 to 2015.
